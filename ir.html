Internal Relations

The Internal Relations committee serves to bring the council, as a whole, together to form a close knit family. We plan numerous social events throughout the year, ranging from the New Member Retreat to the end of the year boat party. We even have an event where we compete with other councils at UT in a friendly game of broomball! If you are creative, want to have fun, and love getting to meet new people, this is the committee for you!
