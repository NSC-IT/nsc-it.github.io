Academic Affairs

Academic Affairs is in charge of listening to the academic concerns of the College of Natural Sciences. We host Town Hall meetings with prominent faculty and student leaders for you to voice your concerns to make CNS a better environment. We recognize excellence in academics and service of professors and students in the college through scholarships and awards.
