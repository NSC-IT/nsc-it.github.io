Community Involvement

Hey, you! Yeah, you. Are you someone who:

  • Likes free T-shirts?
  • Has a soft spot in your heart for vinegar & baking soda volcanoes?
  • Gets a "volunteer's high" from a particularly grueling shift at the local food pantry?
  • Thinks that the only thing better than science...is science outreach?

Community Involvement (CI) is for you! We're the NSC committee that strives to make a difference, and has a GREAT time doing it. You might find us at:

  • Young Scientists: hands-on chemistry & biology experiments with sixth-graders
  • Kids Who Code: teach game programming to eighth-graders
  • UT events, like Longhorn Halloween and Explore UT
  • Community events, like the Austin Marathon or the Regional Science Fai

CI wants you! Join us as we do our small part to make the UT and Austin communities even more fantastic.
