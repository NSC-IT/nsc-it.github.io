Student/Faculty

The student faculty committee fosters the relationship between the students and faculty through various events. We often organize and put together luncheons where the students can interact and get to know professors through an informal setting. However, we also put on a formal banquet in the spring where we honor faculty and staff who have won NSC awards. The events the student faculty committee puts on are meant to mutually benefit the faculty and the students by forming relationships that would otherwise be difficult at such a large university. These relationships often foster, grow, and lead to other great opportunities!
